About Me.

Software Engineer/Data Scientist

I am an enthusiastic leader who consistently meets commitments with integrity. As an analytical, highly organized professional, I thrive in collaborative, team environments as well as independently.

My earliest memories are of passing time in the car on family road trips by making up number games in my head. Only when I got older did I realize I had been re-creating the Fibonacci sequence, factorials, and all things combinatorial. I eventually majored in math at Colgate University then studied as a doctoral student in Computation and Applied Mathematics (I published the very first web pages for the department at the dawn of the web!) at Rice University.
